SignEase

Our Product

Simplify communication between a normal and a deaf person. This product is designed so that it can be understood by anyone.

How it Works

Step 1

Process the input by an algorithm which breaks it down to various components

Step 2

Find the videos of the components and stitch them together in the proper order

Step 3

Stitched seemless video(text to clip) is then delivered to the screen

Areas of Use

During team Presentations, difficulty in understanding the communication between the speech impaired individuals and normal ones can be overcome using this product.
